What is Choose to Create About?

"It is our choices that show what we truly are far more than our abilities."

Choose to create was inspired by all the writing prompt journals floating around in all of the book stores and gift shops of the world. I always picked those books up thinking they were a great idea. I then put them down thinking I could find the same thing on the internet for free.

Choose to create is a website with free unlimited access to all the writing prompts your heart desires.

The idea behind the brand name "choose to create" is to inspire people to create rather than consume. We consume everyday, what is 20 minutes of creating going to hurt?

Write out on your computer or get old school (which is my favorite) and grab that journal you bought years ago with hopes of a new writing habbit, and get to writing :).

Enjoy Creating!
